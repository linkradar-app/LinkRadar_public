FAQ

How do I create an account?
Download the app, enter your email for a magic link or OTP, then complete profile setup (username + photo).
How does anonymous liking work?
Incoming likes appear as a count and yellow dots on the Home radar — not as a list with usernames in Data. Like back from the radar when you see someone nearby.
When do I see someone's @username?
Only after you both liked each other and confirmed bilateral reveal in Data → Mutual. The Matches tab lists people only after reveal is complete.
Can I remove a like or mutual match?
Non-mutual outgoing likes can be removed before they become mutual. Mutual matches cannot be "unliked" — they expire with the like cycle rules or you can block the user.
How do I delete my account?
Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your data from active databases.
How do I export my data?
Settings → Export my data (GDPR). You receive a copy of data we hold about you.
Does LinkRadar store my GPS location?
No precise coordinates on our servers — only zone buckets (10m, 50m, 100m) derived on your device.
How do I block someone?
From Matches after reveal, or contact support. Blocked users won't appear on your radar.
How do I install the Android app?
Use the Download page for the APK. Google Play will be listed there when available.
