Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Data Controller: RES Solutions Ltd. (“we”, “us”, “our”), Bulgaria, European Union.
Privacy contact: vibestudio-res@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how we process personal data when you use the LinkRadar mobile application and related services. LinkRadar is built on privacy by design: we minimize what we collect, we do not store your precise GPS coordinates on our servers, and we only reveal your identity to another user after a mutual match and symmetric reveal by both parties.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect identifiers (email, username, user ID, push token), profile photo URL, age group, zone buckets (10m / 50m / 100m — not lat/long), likes, matches, reveal status, ephemeral reveal chat, blocks, and security-related logs. We do not sell personal information.

2. Location and Proximity

On your device, the OS location services estimate proximity and assign a zone. On our servers we store zone buckets and timestamps — not precise GPS coordinates.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide radar, likes, mutual matches, symmetric reveal, and optional reveal chat
  • Send push notifications (e.g. anonymous “someone liked you” without username in the alert)
  • GDPR export and account deletion in-app
  • Prevent spam, fraud, and abuse

4. Legal Bases (GDPR)

Contract for account and core features; consent for location on device and push; legitimate interests for security where applicable.

5. International Transfers

We use Supabase and Google Firebase Cloud Messaging. Providers may process data outside the EEA with appropriate safeguards.

6. Data Retention

Likes: up to 30 days unless part of a mutual flow. Account: until deletion or extended inactivity. Ephemeral chat: shortly after open (videos one-time view).

7. Your Rights

EEA/UK: access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, object, withdraw consent, complain to a supervisory authority (Bulgaria: CPDP — cpdp.bg).

California: know, delete, correct — we do not sell personal information. Contact vibestudio-res@gmail.com or use in-app Settings.

8. Sharing

Service providers (Supabase, Google/Firebase), payment providers when enabled, and authorities when required by law. Username and photo appear only after mutual bilateral reveal.

9. Security

TLS, access controls, row-level security, and authenticated APIs. No system is fully secure.

10. Children

Not directed at children under 13. Contact us if you believe we collected data from a child without appropriate consent.

11. Changes

We may update this policy and change the “Last Updated” date. We will notify you in the app or by email where required.
